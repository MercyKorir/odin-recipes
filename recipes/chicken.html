Chicken Marinade

Description

This is a great chicken marinade! Allow chicken to marinate for no longer than 4 hours, because the lemon juice will begin to cook the chicken. When grilled, the chicken turns a beautiful caramel color, which makes great presentation.

It has 549 calories; protein being 27.5g; carbohydrates being 7g; fat being 46.1g; cholestrol being 67.2mg; sodium being 1573.9mg

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup olive oil
  • ⅔ cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons liquid smoke flavoring
  • 2 tablespoons spicy brown mustard
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix the olive oil, soy sauce, lemon juice, liquid smoke, mustard, pepper, and garlic powder. Place chicken in the bowl, and coat with the marinade. Cover, and allow chicken to marinate a maximum of 4 hours in the refrigerator.

  2. Preheat the grill for high heat.

  3. Oil grill grate. Discard marinade, and place chicken on grate. Cook 6 to 8 minutes on each side, until juices run clear.

  4. Your Chicken Marinade is now ready to serve :).
